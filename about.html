The Origin

A personal project that grew into a platform.

NYLO started as a question: why do institutional traders consistently outperform, while retail traders struggle with emotion, noise, and timing? The answer wasn't talent — it was infrastructure. Hedge funds run quantitative models with dozens of filters, precise entry criteria, and real-time risk management. Retail traders run on gut feel and Twitter.

Giovanni Flores set out to close that gap. Starting with a simple Python script and a spreadsheet, he reverse-engineered the patterns behind consistent, repeatable edge. After hundreds of backtests, 9 strategy filters, and a walk-forward validation process, NYLO was born — a living algorithmic agent that runs 24/7, watches the market, and surfaces only the highest-probability trades.

The result: 82% win rate across 74 trades, a Sharpe ratio of 4.53, and a profit factor of 1.664 — numbers that hold up in out-of-sample testing.
