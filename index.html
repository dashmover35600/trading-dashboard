How it works

Five filters. One signal.

Every signal passes through five independent confirmation filters before firing. If any filter fails, the signal is skipped. No noise. No guessing.

01
Opening Drive
The first 5 minutes set the day bias. NYLO only takes longs on up-gap days, shorts on down-gap days. Prime window 9:35-10:30 AM gets bonus scoring.
02
VWAP Reclaim
Primary signal: price crosses the Volume Weighted Average Price with above-average volume. Institutional confirmation.
03
EMA Pullback
Secondary signal: price pulls back to the 9 EMA in the trend direction and bounces with momentum.
04
Signal Scoring
Each signal scored 0-10 across 9 factors including momentum, volume direction, prime window, and gap alignment. Score 5+ required. $750-$5,000 position sizing.
05
Smart Exits
Partial exit takes 50% profit at +0.75%. Breakeven stop activates at +0.5%. Trailing stop locks in gains. Daily loss limit protects capital.
06
Pushover Alert
The moment a signal fires you get a push notification with entry, target, stop, and signal score.
